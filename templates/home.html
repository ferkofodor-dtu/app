Instructions

You will be shown images of faces with varying facial expressions.

  • You are asked to rate the smiles of people on a scale of 1 to 5, from sad (1) to happy (5).
  • Submitting a rating will lead you to the next image.
  • There are 400 images in total, a progress bar helps you to see where you are.
  • You can skip / return to images by using the Next and Previous buttons.
  • If you have already rated an image, submitting a new rating will rewrite the old one.
  • You can always reset your session. BEWARE: resetting will restart the whole session and delete your progress.

    • Project Description

    This is a webpage created to facilitate a homework in Cognitive Modelling at DTU. This is Part 1 of a two-part project. First, I am asked to gather ratings of facial expressions on a dataset. I will use these ratings to train a model with which I can generate synthetic facial expressions. In Part 2, I will ask participants to rate the synthetic images to then measure their cognitive biases.


    Disclaimer

    This project is for educational purposes only. The name of all participants will be anonimized and no personal data will be stored.

    The images used in this project are from the UTKFace Dataset (https://susanqq.github.io/UTKFace/).
